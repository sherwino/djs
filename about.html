About Me

I am a Graphic Designer based in Miami, FL. I have been a graphic designer professionally since 2012. I have worked as an in-house designer, as a creative director and product management. I have taken projects from concept to final production and communicated with clients to excute their vision. With an extensive background in digital and print mediums I can meet the needs of any project.                

Experience

As an in-house designer I have created all the graphics need for a company. From printed business cards, flyers, catalogs and brochures. To their entire digital presence. Along with this i have created company presentations and stationary. With this line of work has also come trade shows, where I have designed simple graphics to entire show booths.

Working for an agency I created designs that adhered to various brand style guides. I also experienced going through the approval process needed to publish and finalize designs. I was able to get much experience dealing and working with a wide variety of clients and ideas while still being able to be creative.

Currently working as a creative director and with product management I have worked across both digital and print while also in a new field of product design and development. Creating designs to be used on merchandise to be sold in stores. This new chapter of my career has been very rewarding watching something I created go from concept to prototype to production.      

Skills & Knowledge

I have an expert level knowledge with the Adobe Creative Suite. Well versed in the printing process and preparing projects for print. The technical aspects of designs to be displayed digitally. Creating mock-ups, illustrations, 3D Renders, logo designs and branding.
